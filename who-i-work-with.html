Who I work with

I work with folks who are longing for change and are ready for a different form of embodiment.

Whether you are:

  • craving release from chronic pain or tension
  • pregnant or on a fertility journey and preparing for the transition to parenthood
  • wanting to be a more present and intentional parent
  • on a journey of unmasking and exploring your neurodivergence
  • decolonizing and unlearning dominant oppressive conditioned ways of being
  • being called to come home to your spirit
  • or simply wanting to be more embodied

I can support you as you walk a new path of being.

I feel particularly called to work with folks who are committed to self growth as a part of a larger vision of collective liberation for all, especially those who are BIPOC, queer, neurodivergent, disabled, or otherwise marginalized.
