Who I Work With

I work with folks who are longing for change and are ready for a different form of embodiment.

Whether you are craving release from chronic pain, wanting to shed intergenerational trauma to be a more present parent, exploring your gender, on a journey of unmasking, or being called to come home to your spirit, I can support you as you walk a new path of being.

I feel particularly called to work with folks who are committed to self growth as part of a larger vision of collective liberation for all.

This includes, and is not limited to, those who are BIPOC, queer, neurodivergent, disabled, or otherwise marginalized.
