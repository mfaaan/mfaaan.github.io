Welcome

What is Somatic Bodywork?

Body-based healing

Our bodies carry the stories of our lives, our ancestors, and our communities. Somatic bodywork is a hands-on way of working with and through the body to align with your values, longings, and commitments.

What to expect

Sessions take place fully clothed on a massage table. With touch, breath, movement, and awareness, we explore your unique holding patterns, shapes, and areas of tension to create space to express, release, and open into deeper aliveness.

During sessions

You may experience a wide variety of sensations and emotions. Memories and visions may surface. My role is to support you through whatever arises, at your pace.

Practice Notes

Sessions are collaborative and paced by you. You remain clothed. We can pause at any time for integration or conversation.

Touch is consent-based and negotiated. Your boundaries, cultural context, and lived experience are respected.

Accessibility matters. Please share access needs (fragrances, mobility, sensory, etc.) when booking so I can prepare the space.
