Melissa Anran Fan — Somatic Bodywork

Welcome

Hi, I’m Melissa Anran Fan. I’m a healing arts practitioner, somatic bodyworker, community organizer, mama to 2 kids, and human in process living in service to collective liberation.

I have a MA in Counseling Psychology with a concentration in Expressive Arts Therapy from CIIS and have additional training in somatic therapy, eco therapy, and hypnotherapy. I have worked as a trauma therapist for BIPOC youth in Oakland, a relationship coach for co-housing cooperatives, facilitated retreats for grassroots orgs, and run a variety of educational and healing workshops.

Somatic bodywork is my current offering toward a more loving, open, and just world. After having trained in and offered many different modalities of healing over the years, I have found somatic bodywork to be the most effective at creating lasting change.

What is somatic bodywork?

Our bodies carry the stories of our lives, our ancestors, and our communities. Somatic bodywork is a hands-on way of working directly with and through the body to attune to and align with your values, longings, and commitments.

Sessions take place fully clothed on a massage table. With a combination of touch, breath, movement, and awareness, we gently explore your unique holding patterns, shapes, and areas of tension. Together, we create space for you to express, release, and open into deeper aliveness.

In somatic bodywork, you may experience a wide variety of sensations and emotions. Memories and visions may surface. My role is to support you through whatever arises.
